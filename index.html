Product.

Issue Tracking System

Issue tracking systems fundamentally support the communicative and collaborative aspects of software development, even within small, collocated teams. They do this by providing a robust, canonical, and well compartmentalized database of the work items completed by the development team over time and additionally by storing the discussions and decisions surrounding the issues they contain. In the first section below, we review work that has observed, identified, or examined the social aspects of software development coordination and communication in general and that hint toward the importance of exploring the role of issue tracking systems.

Library Management System

Library Management system allows the Maintenance of all types of Books and CDs within the library through a Proper Channel, Issue-Receipt of Books and CDs. Library Management system also provides different Reports. Books/CDs are maintained by using a Unique numbering system with a code appended to the number which signifies to which category the book/cd belongs to (E.g.. Jav etc. ). This unique number enables you for searching the required book/cd in the library.

Online Feedback System

This system is responsible for getting the feedback of students for each the faculty members. Various types of reports are going to be generated as per feedback. Results also can be exported to excel sheets. The Policy states that student feedback is the responsibility of all staff at here who engage with, and respond to, student feedback and that student feedback will be used to improve the provision of learning resources, facilities, equipment and services through the development of annual improvement plans.

Project Management System

Project Management System is Web-based application, in this system there are three interfaces that are Administrator, Project Scientist, and Apprentice.Admin has whole activities other than assigning the work, and admin can also perform other activities such as add meeting, add back-up, add Project Payment ,changepassword,add Project Document,etc..So, this application is useful for any organization that manages the large number of projects. Many Users can simultaneously use this application.
